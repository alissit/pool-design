About Beemer Pools

Beemer Pools is owned and operated by Brian Moore.

Brian started his business in 1997 by shadowing an experienced pool builder and maintenance contractor. He enjoyed the business so much that he then enrolled in many educational programs to increase his knowledge of the industry and to keep up-to-date with new products as they appeared on the market.

To further his business he then attended and graduated from Lowry School of Chemistry, a highly regarded educator teaching pool chemistry, troubleshooting problems and maintaining proper pool chemistry.

He offers services such as selling, installing & repairing pool equipment, troubleshooting pool problems, installing liners, winterising and spring openings and pool inspections for those who are considering purchasing a home with a pool.

Brian endeavours to educate home owners on the most cost efficient way to operate their pools.

With over 20 years in the industry, Beemer Pools has a very satisfied list of clients.
